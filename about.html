The Horizon Story

Horizon Systems Limited was founded in Singapore in 2017. We started out as a small team who assembled specialized gaming systems and modified mobile devices. Now, five years later we have operations in 195 countries around the world and have sold over 70 million products. Horizon has only begun its journey to provide the most cutting edge computer and mobile technologies on the planet. We remain on the forefront of innovation and creative design as is reflected in our award winning products.
